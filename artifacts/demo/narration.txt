CostRoute Lab uses measured quality and cost rather than intuition to choose a model route. The fixture contains three models across twenty cases for extraction, reasoning, and drafting. With the quality floor set to ninety-one, the exhaustive search finds a route that passes the floor while reducing estimated cost by sixty-one percent against the GPT-5.6-only baseline. Raising the floor beyond the observed scores fails closed. The numbers here are labeled demo observations, not current billing quotes.
